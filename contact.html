Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us

How quickly can you start on my project?

We can typically start within 1-2 weeks after the initial consultation and agreement signing. For urgent projects, we can expedite the process.

What is your project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope. MVPs can be delivered in 4-8 weeks, while enterprise solutions may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you offer ongoing support after deployment?

Yes! We provide maintenance and support packages to ensure your application runs smoothly. This includes bug fixes, security updates, and performance monitoring.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price, time & materials, and monthly retainers. Each project is quoted based on specific requirements and scope.

Do you sign NDAs and protect intellectual property?

Absolutely. We take client confidentiality seriously and are happy to sign NDAs. All intellectual property developed for your project is fully owned by you.
